1. About the internship

1.1 What is the Vicharanashala internship?

expand_more

A two-month internship run by Vicharanashala, a research lab at IIT Ropar. You will work on a real open-source project under a mentor, after a short training phase (which you are already doing). The internship is free — we do not charge, and the work is real.

expand_more

VINS stands for Vicharanashala Internship Network System. It is the primary dashboard and management platform for all internship-related activities, tracking, and communication.

12. ViBe Platform

12.5 I'm experiencing video issues (stuck, looping, skipping) on ViBe. How do I troubleshoot?

expand_more

First, clear your browser cache and cookies. If the issue persists, try accessing ViBe in an Incognito/Private window. Ensure your internet connection is stable. If you are still facing issues, please report it via the Support tab with a screenshot and browser details.

Verified Solution

12.13 I am seeing a red "Access Restricted" banner. Is this a bug?

expand_more

This usually occurs if you are trying to access a module that hasn't been unlocked for your cohort yet, or if your session has expired. Try logging out and logging back in. If the banner remains, check your cohort timeline on the dashboard to ensure the module is scheduled to be open.

Note: Access restrictions are updated daily at 00:00 IST.
